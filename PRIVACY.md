# Privacy Policy

**MobileClaw** — Last updated: March 2, 2026

## Summary

MobileClaw does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app is a client that connects to servers you configure on your own network.

## Data Collection

MobileClaw collects **no data**. Specifically:

- No analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting
- No advertising or tracking
- No cookies or fingerprinting
- No account creation required

## Network Connections

MobileClaw only connects to servers that you explicitly configure in the app settings (e.g., a local LM Studio instance or OpenClaw gateway). No connections are made to any other servers.

All communication happens directly between your device and your configured server. No data passes through any intermediary.

## On-Device Storage

The app stores your settings (server URL, selected model) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

MobileClaw uses no third-party services, SDKs, or analytics frameworks.

## Children's Privacy

MobileClaw does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at https://github.com/wende/mobileclaw/issues.
